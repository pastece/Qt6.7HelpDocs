Qt Spatial Audio QML Types

The QML types for Qt Spatial Audio support the full functionality of the C++ API.

QML Types

Qt Spatial Audio QML types are designed to be used together with Qt Quick 3D. They can be imported into your application using the following import statement in your .qml file:

 import QtQuick3D.SpatialAudio

AmbientSound

A stereo overlay sound

AudioEngine

Manages sound objects inside a 3D scene

AudioListener

Defines the position and orientation of the person listening to a sound field defined by a AudioEngine

AudioRoom

SpatialSound

A sound object in 3D space
